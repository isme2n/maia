# Part 5 OSS Public Surface Matrix

This matrix is the Part 5 source of truth for Maia's open-source public reading order.

- `primary`: the first public surface for that audience. It should be the default entrypoint.
- `secondary`: public and supported, but not the first thing that should carry the story.
- `internal-only`: allowed in the repo, but must not lead the OSS product story.

Part 5 does not replace the existing command contracts from Part 4 bootstrap or Part 2 Keryx. It fixes where those contracts belong for OSS readers and contributors.

## Notes

- Part 4 remains the source of truth for the bootstrap command contract. Part 5 only says that bootstrap belongs in the primary OSS quickstart rather than being mixed with contributor or internal narrative.
- Part 2 Keryx remains the source of truth for `/keryx`, `thread`, `handoff`, `workspace`, and `delivery_mode`. Part 5 only fixes the OSS presentation order: product and concepts first, operator support surfaces second.
- Internal or dev-only material is not forbidden from the repository. The Part 5 rule is placement: keep it out of the primary README story and out of the top-level help summary.
